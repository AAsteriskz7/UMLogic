## 1. Introduction
UMLogic is designed to help students learn, build, and connect UML diagrams through interactive modules, step-by-step guides, and quizzes. All content is based on the CampusConnect scenario, a realistic case study for student organization and event management.

## 2. Getting Started
- Visit the live site: [umlogic.vercel.app](https://umlogic.vercel.app)
- No account required; your progress is saved locally in your browser.
- Recommended browsers: Chrome, Edge, Firefox.

## 3. Navigation & UI Overview
- **Sidebar**: Access diagram modules, dashboard, quizzes, and sandbox.
- **Dashboard**: Overview of your progress and quick links to modules.
- **Dark Mode**: Toggle for comfortable viewing.
- **Smooth Animations**: Framer Motion powers transitions for a premium feel.

## 4. Learning Modules & Diagrams
Each module includes:
- **Purpose**: What the diagram is for.
- **Process**: Step-by-step construction, with animated scrubber.
- **Connection**: How it relates to other diagrams and the CampusConnect scenario.

### Use Case Diagram (UCD)
- Covers scenarios 1, 2, and 3.
- Focus: Actors (Student, President, Officer), system boundaries.
- Learn how to identify use cases and relationships.

### Domain Model Diagram (DMD)
- Based on CampusConnect context.
- Focus: Conceptual classes and relationships.
- Build a model of organizations, students, events, and their connections.

### System Sequence Diagram (SSD)
- Scenario 3: Priya checks events.
- Focus: External interactions between actors and the system.
- Visualize system responses to user actions.

### Sequence Diagram (SD)
- Scenario 2: Daniel creates an event and RSVP logic.
- Focus: Internal logic, object creation, capacity validation.
- Step through the RSVP process and event management.

### Design Class Diagram (DCD)
- Scenario 2.
- Focus: Attributes, methods, multiplicities.
- See how classes are designed for implementation.

## 5. Interactive Features
- **Animated Step-by-Step Scrubber**: Slide through diagram construction.
- **Live Diagram Rendering**: Powered by Mermaid.js for instant updates.
- **Interactive Nodes**: Click elements for explanations and tips.

## 6. Progress Tracking
- Your learning progress and quiz scores are saved in browser localStorage.
- Dashboard shows completed modules and quiz results.

## 7. Quizzes & Sandbox Mode
- **Quizzes**: Test your understanding for each diagram type. Each module includes a mini quiz with multiple-choice and interactive questions. Your scores are tracked on the dashboard, helping you identify areas for improvement.
- **Proficiency Quiz**: After completing all modules, take the comprehensive proficiency quiz. This covers all diagram types and CampusConnect scenarios. Scoring is immediate, and a passing score unlocks a completion badge on your dashboard.
- **Sandbox**: Experiment with building your own diagrams using Mermaid syntax. The sandbox features a professional-grade editor:
    - Write Mermaid code and see live diagram previews.
    - Export diagrams as PNG images for use in assignments or reports.
    - Use the "Export" button to download your diagram.
    - Try different diagram types (UCD, DMD, SSD, SD, DCD) and customize elements.

Tips:
- Use the sandbox to practice and reinforce concepts from the modules.
- Share exported diagrams with teammates or instructors for feedback.
- The sandbox is ideal for creative exploration and advanced learning.

## 8. FAQ & Troubleshooting
- **Q:** Why can’t I see my progress on another device?
  - **A:** Progress is saved locally; use the same browser and device.
- **Q:** Diagrams not rendering?
  - **A:** Refresh the page or check browser compatibility.
- **Q:** How do I reset my progress?
  - **A:** Use the dashboard reset option.
